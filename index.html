Businessman Turned Development Geek

Sitting alone in a lonely hotel room, I reflected on where my career was taking me. I was a sales rep for an investment accounting software company. One of my responsibilities included traveling to the East Coast to meet with prospective clients and teach them how our software could improve their lives. Because of this automated software, I could offer my prospects something that would allow them to spend less time doing manual work and instead give them more time away from the office.

Being able to give people their lives back was wonderful! However, my job was requiring more and more time from me. I was traveling nearly every week and would be gone multiple days at a time. I liked helping people, but I didn’t like how my job was affecting my family. I wondered if I could go back in time and pick a different career, what would I pursue? In that moment of reflection, I wished I had become a software developer. However, without access to a time machine, I thought it was too late for me to make a career change.

Fast forward a few months. There were some unexpected changes at work and I started looking for new employment. I was taken back to the thoughts I had in my hotel room about becoming a software developer. However, I “knew” it was too late for me to make a change. I happened to mention this to a friend of mine and luckily, he told me about coding boot camps. I studied up on them and learned that it wasn’t too late for me to make a career change after all! After a lot of consideration, I decided attending a boot camp was the right thing for me.

I’m extremely grateful I made this decision. In just a few short months, I’ve developed a passion for coding. I look forward to my new career and would love to help you and your organization.
